About ABABEEL FINSOL PRO
Complete feature guide and documentation
💎
ABABEEL FINSOL PRO
Version 3.0 · Personal Finance Pro · Developed by Mubarak
A complete personal finance management system with multi-account support, stock investment tracking, budget planning, savings goals, and advanced analytics. All data is stored securely in your browser — no cloud, no server, fully private.
Feature Guide
🏦
Multiple Bank Accounts — Add Any Bank
Go to Bank Accounts in the sidebar and click "+ Add Account". Enter the account name (e.g. YES Bank, Indian Overseas Bank, SBI, Cash Wallet, Groww Wallet), choose the type (Bank / Cash / Digital Wallet), and set an opening balance. Each account tracks its own running balance independently. The Dashboard Net Worth bar shows ALL accounts combined and lists each one individually with its exact full balance (no abbreviation). You can add as many accounts as you need — there is no limit.
🔄
Transfer (Contra) Between Any Two Accounts
When adding an entry, select type Transfer (Contra). Two dropdowns appear: From Account and To Account. You can select any two of your saved accounts — for example, transfer from YES Bank to Indian Overseas Bank, or from Bank to Cash. The system records a double-entry: Debit on the source account + Credit on the destination account. This keeps both account balances perfectly accurate without affecting overall net worth.
📁
Bank Statement Import — Your Bank's Format
The import parser supports the exact format: Tran Date, Chq No, Particulars, Debit, Credit, Balance, Init.Br. Also supports other common formats: Date / Transaction Date / Value Date, Narration / Description / Remarks, Withdrawal Amount / Deposit Amount. Before importing: (1) Select which account to import into. (2) Click Browse Files or drag & drop. (3) Review the preview table. (4) Click Import All. Amounts with commas (e.g. 21,642.00) are automatically cleaned and parsed correctly.
📊
Analytics — 4 Chart Types Including Stock-Style
Monthly P&L: Bar chart comparing income vs expense per month. By Category: Horizontal bar chart ranking your top spending categories. Savings Trend: Line chart showing cumulative savings growing over time. Stock-Style Chart: Each month shown as green bar (surplus) or red bar (deficit) — exactly like financial market charts. Hover for exact values.
🕌
Charity / Zakat Category
When adding an expense entry, the category quick-pills include Charity / Zakat and Sadaqah as dedicated options. These appear in the spending breakdown chart and in the budget planner — so you can set a monthly Charity / Zakat budget and track how much you have given.
💰
Full Balance Display — No Abbreviation (K / L)
All balances are displayed in full Indian number format — for example ₹21,642.00 not ₹21.6K. This applies to the Net Worth bar, all KPI cards, account cards, ledger balance column, and stock P&L. No confusion or rounding errors for large balances.
📈
Stock Market Investments — Completely Separate Treatment
The Stock Market section tracks investments with its own dedicated ledger and KPIs, completely separate from personal finance. How to use: (1) Record a BUY trade: enter symbol (e.g. RELIANCE, TCS, NIFTY50), quantity, buy price, broker. (2) Record a SELL trade: enter sell price and original buy price — Realized P&L = (Sell − Buy) × Qty. (3) View KPIs: Total Invested, Total Sell Value, Realized P&L (green/red), Win Rate %. (4) Stock-style bar chart shows monthly P&L in green/red bars.
📋
Budget Planner
Set monthly spending limits per category. The system calculates how much you have spent this calendar month in each category and shows a color progress bar: Green = within budget, Red = over budget. Useful categories to budget: Food, Transport, Rent, Fuel, Medical, Charity / Zakat, Education, Entertainment, EMI.
🔄
Receivables, Payables & Commitments
Receivables: Money owed TO you. Click "Received" to auto-create a bank credit entry in the ledger. Payables: Money you OWE. Click "Paid" to auto-create a bank debit. Commitments: Reminders — EMI due dates, subscription renewals, financial meetings. All overdue items (past due date and still open) are automatically highlighted in red.
💾
Backup & Restore
Click BACKUP in the top bar or Settings to download a full JSON backup of all data (accounts, entries, stocks, goals, budgets, etc.). Restore on any device using the Restore Backup button. Export ledger as CSV for Excel. Data is stored in browser localStorage — clearing browser data will erase it, so regular backups are strongly recommended.
