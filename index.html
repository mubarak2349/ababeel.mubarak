ABABEEL FINSOL PRO
Version 6.0 · Multi-User Personal Finance + 10 New Features
Complete personal finance management with secure multi-user login, separate private data per user, multi-account support, stock investment tracking, budget planning, savings goals, advanced analytics, EMI calculator, Zakat calculator, currency converter, recurring transactions, financial health score, quick calculator, notes, date range filter, and print report.

Multi-User Login — Completely Separate Data
Each account has its own private data. Admin: admin / ababeel123. Guest uses isolated namespace.

NEW: Financial Health Score
Get a 0–100 score based on savings rate, expense control, goal progress and account diversity. Shown on your dashboard.

NEW: Recurring Transactions
Schedule monthly/weekly/yearly recurring income and expenses. Post them to your ledger with one click.

NEW: Quick Calculator (FAB)

📝
NEW: Financial Notes
Save memos, reminders, tax notes, and ideas tagged by category.
💱
NEW: Currency Converter
Convert between 10 major currencies with approximate reference rates. Swap currencies with one click.

NEW: Zakat Calculator
Calculate your annual Zakat (2.5%) on eligible wealth above the Nisab threshold. Enter cash, gold, investments, receivables.
🏦
NEW: EMI / Loan Calculator
Calculate monthly EMI, total interest, and view an amortization schedule for reducing balance or flat rate loans.
📅
NEW: Date Range Filter in Ledger
Filter ledger transactions by a custom date range (from → to) in addition to existing search filters.

Print your full financial dashboard using browser print. Sidebar, buttons and UI elements are hidden for a clean report.
