🔐
Multi-User Login — Completely Separate Data
Each account has its own private data. Admin: admin / ababeel123. Guest uses isolated namespace.

Stock Market — Buy & Sell from Holdings
Add BUY trades. Open positions show automatically. Click SELL button next to any holding to book profit/loss at your rate. Realized P&L = (Sell − Buy) × Qty.

Bank Balance on Dashboard
All account balances shown as cards at top of dashboard. Net Worth = sum of all account balances.
🕐
Indian Standard Time Clock
Live IST clock shown in the topbar with greeting based on actual time of day.
